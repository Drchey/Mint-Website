The Nigerian
Mint

The Nigerian Security Printing and Minting PLC is the foremost and only full-fledged
security printing organisation in the African sub-region.
It was established in 1963 by the government of the Federal Republic of Nigeria in partnership with De La Rue of the U.K
The company is wholly managed and operated by Nigerians

Our Services

International Passports
The MINT produces International Passports. We leverage our deep knowledge of secure printing in making these documents See more
Currency Production
The MINT has been in charge of the production of local currency notes since they were introduced in 1965. It does this on behalf of the Central Bank of Nigeria. See more
Certificates
The MINT produces imitation-proof certificates for its clients in various industries. These include education and the academia, banking and financial investment, and real estate sectors. See more
Examinination Question Paper
The MINT prints examination papers for various examining bodies in Nigeria. It also provides full range security solutions for examination papers, covering every stage up until after their use. See more
Ballot Paper
Ballot papers are an important part of the electoral process. They bear the mandate that citizens of the country give to their preferred candidate for public office. See more
Corporate quality policy
